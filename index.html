About Trueglam Pharmaceuticals

Founded in 2023, Trueglam Pharmaceuticals Private Limited (CIN: U46497DL2023PTC411141) was incorporated on 16 March 2023. Our Registered Office is at House No. 1964/7, 3rd Floor, Kingsway Camp, Outram Line (Near Hari Mandir), GTB Nagar, New Delhi – 110009, India.

We operate as a wholesale pharmaceutical distributor, connecting manufacturers to hospitals, clinics and pharmacies. Trueglam is led by a team of experienced professionals; our Board of Directors includes Umesh Chand Garg, Renu Sharma, Saurabh Bansal, and Atul Sharma.

Our core mission, "Caring for Life," guides every decision at Trueglam. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of regulatory compliance and product integrity. In line with India's regulatory framework, every distribution activity is conducted under the principles of Good Distribution Practices (GDP).

We maintain state-of-the-art warehousing and transport systems (temperature control, secure packaging, traceability, etc.) to safeguard drug quality. These efforts reflect global best practices: as WHO notes, an effective quality assurance system "must be founded on a reliable system of controlling the quality, safety and efficacy of a finished product delivered to a market".

By rigorously enforcing such standards, Trueglam ensures that all products we distribute – from premium supplements to essential antibiotics – arrive in perfect condition and comply with the Drugs and Cosmetics Act and other regulations.
